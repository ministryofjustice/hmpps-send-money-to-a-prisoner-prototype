Question about a payment I want to make or am trying to make

Can you tell me the prisoner number or their date of birth?

No. By law we can't give out confidential prisoner information. Ask the prisoner for these details.

How do I make a payment from abroad?

You can only make a payment from outside the UK using a debit card (Visa, Mastercard or Maestro). We don't accept credit cards or prepaid debit cards.

Can I send money to a Scottish prison?

No. Our service is only available in England and Wales. Call the Scottish prison directly or go to the [Scottish Prison Service].

Question about a payment made in the past

Can I cancel a payment?

We can't cancel your payment because once it's gone through the system, it's non-refundable.

Will my payment be transferred if a prisoner moves prisons?

Your payment will be transferred to the new prison but it may take a few days.

Can I get a refund if the prisoner has been released?

Yes. Contact the prison and they’ll tell you what to do.

You didn’t ask which prison it is - does it matter?

No. The name of the prison does not affect the use of this service.

My payment went through but isn’t in the prisoner’s account - why not?

It can only be one of 2 things.

One - If you paid by bank transfer, you may have made a mistake typing in the prisoner reference number. You will get an automatic refund around 4 days after you made the payment.

Check for this refund in your bank account, then use the service again, carefully checking the details as you type them in.

Two - if you paid by bank transfer from abroad, by CHAPS or in a bank branch in the UK, you'll need to ask the prison for a refund.

To get a refund, write to the finance department at the prison.

Include in the letter:

  • the prisoner's name, their prisoner number and their date of birth
  • your name and address
  • the payment date and the amount

The prison will refund you by cheque.

Why has my payment been refunded?

You've got a refund because when you made your bank transfer, a piece of information was wrong or missing. Please try again, checking that you're using the right details.

How does a prisoner know they’ve received a payment?

The prisoner might get an account slip from the wing officer when a payment has reached their account, but this can take a few days and not all prisons use these slips.Ask the prisoner to check with their wing officer if the money has reached their account. But payments take up to 3 days after you sent it.

Does a prisoner know who a payment is from?

A prisoner will see the sender’s name on their account slip, but not all prisons give out these slips.

I’m worried about the safety of a prisoner I’ve sent a payment to - what should I do?

If you’re worried about a prisoner, contact the prison and tell them. They will put you through to the Safer Custody team or an orderly officer. Use [find a prisoner](https://www.gov.uk/find-prisoner) if you need to check what prison they're in.
