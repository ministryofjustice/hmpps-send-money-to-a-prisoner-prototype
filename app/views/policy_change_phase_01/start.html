You can use this service to make a payment by Visa, Mastercard or Maestro debit card. Money usually takes less than 3 working days to reach a prisoner’s account, but it may take longer.

This service is free and secure, and available in all prisons in England and Wales.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll need the:

  • prisoner’s date of birth
  • prisoner number

Help using the service

You can get help with making a payment, ask a question about a payment you've already made, or get help with accessing the service.

Other ways to send money

From 2 November 2020 you will no longer be able to send money by bank transfer, cheque, postal order or send cash by post to any prison. You may be able to get help if you cannot use the online service to pay.

You can also use the service to find out how to make a UK bank transfer to a prisoner by:

  • online banking
  • mobile banking app
  • telephone banking

If you do not have a bank account

You can also send a postal order, cheque or cash to a prisoner.
