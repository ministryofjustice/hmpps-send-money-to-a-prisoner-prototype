Service change from November 2nd

From November 2nd, you will no longer be able to use this page to send money by bank transfer.

You will not be able to send in cash, cheques or postal orders by post either.

This service change will mean the best way to send money to someone in prison will be by debit card.

This change is to keep everyone who lives and works in prisons safe. If it means you can no longer use this service, there are alternatives.

Get a prisoner reference to make your UK bank transfer

You’ll get a prisoner reference so you can make a bank transfer using:

  • online banking
  • mobile banking app
  • telephone banking

You can re-use this reference for this prisoner each time you make a transfer. The money should take around 3 days to reach the prisoner’s account. You won’t get email confirmation after your bank transfer.

You can't

  • go into a bank branch to transfer money
  • transfer money from a bank account outside the UK
  • make a payment by CHAPS

Paying by debit card is fast, simple, reliable and free and the best way to send money to someone in prison.
