You can use this service to make a payment by Visa, Mastercard or Maestro debit card.

This service is free and secure, and available in all public prisons in England and Wales.

You cannot use the service to send money to someone in a private prison except HMP Lowdham Grange, HMP Northumberland and HMP Oakwood.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll need the:

  • prisoner’s date of birth
  • prisoner number

Money usually takes around 3 working days to reach a prisoner’s account.

Other ways to make a payment

You can also use it to find out how to make a UK bank transfer to a prisoner by:

  • online banking
  • mobile banking app
  • telephone banking
Your money will not reach the prisoner if you make a transfer from a bank branch or non-UK account.

You need a bank account to use this service. If you do not have one you can send money to a prisoner using a postal order, a cheque or cash.

Help using the service

You can get help if you’ve already sent money but it has not reached the prisoner yet.

You can also contact the support team if you have any other questions about using the service.
