    No. By law we can't give out confidential prisoner information. Ask the prisoner for these details.

    How do I make a payment from abroad?

    You can only make a payment from outside the UK using a debit card (Visa, Mastercard or Maestro). We don't accept credit cards or prepaid debit cards.

    Can I send money to a Scottish prison?

    No. Our service is only available in England and Wales. Call the Scottish prison directly or go to the [Scottish Prison Service].
