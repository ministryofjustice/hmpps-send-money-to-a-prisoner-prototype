Frequently asked questions

Check if the answer to your question is here already before contacting us

A payment has taken longer than expected

In almost all cases, the payment reaches the prisoner’s bank account in up to 3 days. However, if it seems to be taking longer, it could be because an individual prison has put a payment on hold or the money could be in the prisoner’s account but the prisoner doesn’t know about it yet. If you’re still worried after 7-10 days, please contact the prison.

A prisoner has been moved or released

    If the prisoner has been:
    • moved, the money will be transferred to the next prison, whether it’s a public or private prison – if it’s a private prison the process may take longer
    • released, they need to contact the prison and ask the prison to send them the money
