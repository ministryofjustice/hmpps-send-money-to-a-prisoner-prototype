Get a prisoner reference to use in a UK bank transfer

You can make a UK bank transfer to a prisoner by:

  • online banking
  • mobile banking app
  • telephone banking

Your money will not reach the prisoner if you make a transfer from a bank branch or non-UK account.

  1. You’ll get a prisoner reference you can use to make a bank transfer using online banking, your mobile banking app or telephone banking
  2. The money should take up to 3 working days to reach the prisoner’s account
  3. You don’t get email confirmation after your bank transfer

Because it is the easiest and most reliable way of getting money to a prisoner, it is recommended that you pay by debit card
