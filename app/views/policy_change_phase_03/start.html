You can use this service to make a payment by Visa, Mastercard or Maestro debit card. Money usually takes less than 3 working days to reach a prisoner’s account, but it may take longer.

This service is free and secure, and available in all prisons in England and Wales.

Before you start

You’ll need the:

  • prisoner’s date of birth
  • prisoner number

Help using the service

You can get help with making a payment or with a payment already made.

If you do not have a debit card

You can find out how to get one by setting up a bank account.

If you do not have access to this service

You can apply for an exemption which, in exceptional circumstances, might allow you to send money in through the post.
