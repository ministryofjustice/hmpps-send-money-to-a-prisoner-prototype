Service changes from November 2nd

A policy change on November 2nd will mean the best way to send money to someone in prison will be by debit card.

You will no longer be able to use this service to send money by bank transfer. And you won't be able to send in cash, cheques or postal orders.

The policy change is to keep everyone who lives and works in prisons safe.

If you can not use this service or don't have a bank account, there are alternatives.

Pay now by debit card

Paying by debit card is the fastest, simplest and most reliable way of sending money to a prisoner - and it’s free

You can complete your payment here using Mastercard, Visa and Maestro debit cards. Money usually takes less than 3 working days to reach a prisoner’s account, but may take longer.

You’ll get email confirmation of payment.

You can make an international payment using a debit card.

From January 2020, you will no longer be able to use a pre-paid debit card.
