From November 2nd some changes will be made to this service

A policy change on November 2nd will mean the best way to send money to someone in prison will be by debit card, using this service.

You will no longer be able to use this service to send money by bank transfer. And you won't be able to send in cash, cheques or Postal Orders.

If you can not use this service or don't have a bank account, there are alternatives to this service.

Get a prisoner reference to make your UK bank transfer

You’ll get a prisoner reference so you can make a bank transfer using:

  • online banking
  • mobile banking app
  • telephone banking

You can re-use this reference for this prisoner each time you make a transfer. The money should take around 3 days to reach the prisoner’s account. You won’t get email confirmation after your bank transfer.

You can't

  • go into a bank branch to transfer money
  • transfer money from a bank account outside the UK
  • make a payment by CHAPS

Paying by debit card is the fastest, simplest and most reliable way of sending money to a prisoner - and it’s free
